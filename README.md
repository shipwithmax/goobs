# Goobs

A free open-source video editor with the editorial DNA of a professional editor baked in. Built by [MaxShip](https://max-ship.com).

> **Status: pre-alpha scaffold.** Real code coming soon. Watch this repo or follow [@shipwithmax](https://x.com/shipwithmax) for the launch.

## What Goobs is

Most people don't know how to edit video. They have great content trapped behind technical friction — they can't film, edit, and publish in a day. Goobs removes that friction.

You give Goobs raw footage and a rough idea. Goobs uses the editorial principles of a professional editor — encoded into its prompts and defaults — to produce a publishable video that doesn't feel like AI slop. You stay in creative control; Goobs handles the craft.

## What's inside

- **Pre-production helper** — figures out what you should talk about before you film
- **AI-assisted editor** — built on free, open-source [Hyperframes](https://github.com/hyperframes) (or similar HTML-based video editing). Pro AI prompts produce the edits.
- **Social scheduler** — edit and schedule in one flow
- **Editorial DNA** — actual answers from a working pro editor, encoded into the tool's prompts and behaviors
